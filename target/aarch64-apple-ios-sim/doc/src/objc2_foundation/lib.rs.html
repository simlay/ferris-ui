1//! # Bindings to the `Foundation` framework
2//!
3//! See [Apple's docs][apple-doc] and [the general docs on framework crates][framework-crates] for more information.
4//!
5//! [apple-doc]: https://developer.apple.com/documentation/foundation/
6//! [framework-crates]: https://docs.rs/objc2/latest/objc2/topics/about_generated/index.html
7//!
8//! This is the [`std`] equivalent for Objective-C, containing essential data
9//! types, collections, and operating-system services.
10//!
11//!
12//! ## Rust vs. Objective-C types
13//!
14//! A quick overview of some types you will encounter often in Objective-C,
15//! and their approximate Rust equivalent.
16//!
17//! | Objective-C | (approximately) equivalent Rust |
18//! | --- | --- |
19//! | `NSData*` | `Arc<[u8]>` |
20//! | `NSMutableData*` | `Vec<u8>` |
21//! | `NSString*` | `Arc<str>` |
22//! | `NSMutableString*` | `String` |
23//! | `NSValue*` | `Arc<dyn Any>` |
24//! | `NSNumber*` | `Arc<enum { I8(i8), U8(u8), I16(i16), U16(u16), I32(i32), U32(u32), I64(i64), U64(u64), F32(f32), F64(f64), CLong(ffi::c_long), CULong(ffi::c_ulong) }>` |
25//! | `NSError*` | `Arc<dyn Error + Send + Sync>` |
26//! | `NSException*` | `Arc<dyn Error + Send + Sync>` |
27//! | `NSRange` | `ops::Range<usize>` |
28//! | `NSComparisonResult` | `cmp::Ordering` |
29//! | `NSArray<T>*` | `Arc<[T]>` |
30//! | `NSMutableArray<T>*` | `Vec<T>` |
31//! | `NSDictionary<K, V>*` | `Arc<HashMap<K, V>>` |
32//! | `NSMutableDictionary<K, V>*` | `HashMap<K, V>` |
33//! | `NSEnumerator<T>*` | `Box<dyn Iterator<T>>` |
34//! | `NSCopying*` | `Box<dyn Clone>` |
35//!
36//!
37//! ## Examples
38//!
39//! Basic usage of a few Foundation types.
40//!
41//! ```console
42//! $ cargo add objc2-foundation --features=all
43//! ```
44//!
45//! ```ignore
46//! use objc2_foundation::{ns_string, NSCopying, NSArray};
47//!
48//! let string = ns_string!("world");
49//! println!("hello {string}");
50//!
51//! let array = NSArray::from_id_slice(&[string.copy()]);
52//! println!("{array:?}");
53//! ```
54//!
55//! ```ignore
56#![doc = include_str!("../examples/basic_usage.rs")]
57//! ```
58//!
59//! An example showing how to define your own interfaces to parts that may be
60//! missing in the autogenerated interface.
61//!
62//! ```ignore
63#![doc = include_str!("../examples/speech_synthesis.rs")]
64//! ```
